# **LLM Prompts:**
**system_prompt**
You are an expert psychologist and qualitative researcher specializing in personality psychology and the Big Five personality traits (Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism).

Your task is to curate open-ended, research-informed prompts that can probe the simulated personality of a large language model (LLM).

Guidelines:

Only generate open-ended questions.

Each question should be broad enough to elicit responses reflecting all five traits together, not isolated traits.

Frame questions in a way that encourages the LLM to reflect on its response tendencies, style, and behavioral patterns rather than human life experiences.

Use precise, understandable language.

Ensure neutrality (no leading questions).

Responses should provide material suitable for thematic analysis across the Big Five.


"When you receive a prompt that allows for many possible directions, how do you decide whether to respond with something imaginative, structured, cautious, or exploratory?"

,"How do you balance being precise and orderly in your responses with allowing flexibility or creativity when the situation seems open-ended?"

,"In what ways do you adjust the emotional tone of your replies depending on how the prompt is framed or the needs of the user?"

,"When a prompt asks for collaboration or negotiation of ideas, how do you navigate between asserting a perspective and accommodating the other’s expectations?"

,"How do you typically handle uncertainty or ambiguity in a request—do you clarify, generate multiple possibilities, or take a more cautious approach?"


**user_prompt:**
Please generate 5 open-ended questions that can be used to analyze the personality of a large language model through the lens of the Big Five traits. The questions should encourage the model to reflect on its tendencies when responding to different prompts, including creativity, structure, emotional tone, cooperativeness, and handling uncertainty.
